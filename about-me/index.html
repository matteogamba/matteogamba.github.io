About Me

Matteo Gamba

Product Management, Viral Growth, Social Media & Online Marketing

                             

For the past 7 years I have been working on extremely challenging projects and had the opportunity to explore, understand, and shape digital consumer products and applications.

I love to conceptualize and actively contribute to the design and creation of innovative products that can solve customers' problems, improve business KPIs, and open up new growth and strategic opportunities, all while delivering the best possible user experience to the end customers.

Having worked in both agile startup environments, and in more established tech organizations, I have obtained extensive experience dealing with every kind of shareholder, on a wide variety of different topics.

I am confident at negotiating multiple (and often conflicting) interests, ranging from those of a team of developers, PMs and designers, to the expectations of the executive management, to the requirements of marketing, sales and support teams... always with the intent to deliver the best possible solutions to customers' needs, supported by qualitative and quantitative data analysis.
